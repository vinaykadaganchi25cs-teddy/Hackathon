🧘 Yoga Postures

🐱

Cat-Cow Stretch

On hands and knees, alternate arching and rounding your back. Relieves spine tension and improves flexibility.

Easy · 2 min
🧒

Child's Pose

Kneel and stretch arms forward on the floor. A restorative pose that releases back, hip, and shoulder tension.

Easy · 1 min
🔄

Seated Spinal Twist

Sit and twist your torso gently. Can be done in a chair! Improves spinal mobility and digestion.

Easy · 1 min
⛰️

Mountain Pose

Stand tall, feet together, arms at sides. Focus on alignment and breathing. Resets your posture baseline.

Easy · 1 min
🐕

Downward Dog

Inverted V-shape. Stretches hamstrings, calves, and shoulders while strengthening arms.

Medium · 2 min
🦅

Eagle Arms

Cross arms and wrap forearms. Opens upper back and shoulders — perfect after long computer sessions.

Easy · 1 min

💧 Hydration Guide

🥤

8 Glasses Daily

Aim for 2 liters (8 glasses) of water per day. Set hourly reminders. Use a marked water bottle to track intake.

Essential
🍋

Infused Water

Add lemon, cucumber, or mint to make water more appealing. Increases your likelihood of staying hydrated by 40%.

Tip
☕

Limit Caffeine

Coffee dehydrates. For every cup of coffee, drink an extra glass of water. Switch to green tea after 2 PM.

Important

🪑 Posture-Fixing Exercises

🔽

Chin Tucks

Pull chin straight back (making a double chin). Hold 5 seconds, repeat 10x. Corrects forward head posture from screens.

Easy · 2 min
🧱

Wall Angels

Stand with back against wall, slide arms up and down like making snow angels. Opens chest and strengthens upper back.

Medium · 3 min
🤸

Chest Opener

Clasp hands behind back, squeeze shoulder blades together, lift arms. Counteracts hunched desk posture.

Easy · 1 min
🦵

Hip Flexor Stretch

Kneel on one knee, push hips forward. Hold 30 seconds each side. Reverses hip tightness from sitting.

Medium · 3 min
🌉

Glute Bridge

Lie on back, knees bent, lift hips. Activates glutes that go dormant from prolonged sitting.

Medium · 3 min
🚶

Walk 5 Minutes Every Hour

Research shows a 5-minute walk every hour reduces cardiovascular risk by 58% and boosts productivity by 15%. Set your break timer to 60 minutes in the Live Monitor!
